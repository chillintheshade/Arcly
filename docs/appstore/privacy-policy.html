Privacy Policy for Arcly

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Arcly ("the App") is a macOS application launcher developed by Dongyang Wang. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Summary

Arcly does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App runs entirely on your Mac. There are no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and no servers operated by the developer.

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect any information from you or your device.

Data Stored Locally

To function, Arcly saves your preferences — such as the apps you have added to the launcher, shortcut keys, and appearance settings — on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your Mac and is not accessible to the developer.

In-App Purchases

Arcly offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Arcly Pro") to unlock additional features. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your name, email, payment details, or any other personally identifiable information. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to these transactions.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

System Permissions

Arcly may request standard macOS permissions required for launcher functionality (for example, accessing apps you explicitly select, or controlling other apps via Apple Events). These permissions are used solely to provide in-app features and are never used to collect data.

Children's Privacy

Arcly is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
